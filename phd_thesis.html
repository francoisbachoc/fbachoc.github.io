Habilitation thesis (HDR)

I defended my habilitation thesis, "Contributions to Gaussian processes, uncertainty quantification and post-model-selection inference" on November 29, 2018, at University Paul Sabatier, before the jury composed by:

  • Vincent RIVOIRARD (president), Université Paris Dauphine
  • Jean-Michel LOUBES (coordinator), Université Paul Sabatier
  • Emilio PORCU (reviewer), University of Newcastle
  • Clémentine PRIEUR (reviewer), Université Grenoble Alpes
  • Bernard BERCU (examiner), Université Bordeaux 1
  • Béatrice LAURENT (examiner), Université Paul Sabatier

The thesis was reviewed by:

  • Gerda CLAESKENS, KU Leuven
  • Emilio PORCU, University of Newcastle
  • Clémentine PRIEUR, Université Grenoble Alpes

Ph.D thesis

  • Dominique PICARD (president), Université Paris. Diderot
  • Josselin GARNIER (PhD advisor), Université Paris. Diderot
  • Jean-Marc MARTINEZ (CEA advisor), CEA Saclay
  • Luc PRONZATO (reviewer), Université de Nice-Sophia Antipolis
  • Hannes LEEB (examiner), Universität Wien
  • Nicolas VAYATIS (examiner), ENS Cachan
  • Emmanuel VAZQUEZ (examiner), École supérieure d'électricité

The thesis was reviewed by:

  • Luc PRONZATO, Université de Nice-Sophia Antipolis
  • Michael STEIN, University of Chicago
